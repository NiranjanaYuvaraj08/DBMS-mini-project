About Staff Dashboard

The Staff Dashboard provides an easy interface for managing various student-related data such as registration, courses, attendance, and performance. Staff can also add departments and manage staff-related tasks.

Features

  • Add and manage student records.

  • Track attendance and academic progress.

  • Assign and manage courses and exams.

  • Monitor department activities and staff assignments.
